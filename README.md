# 📊 Sales Dashboard – Power BI Project

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an **interactive Sales Dashboard** built using **Power BI** to analyze and visualize sales data. The dashboard provides insights into overall business performance, sales trends, customer activity, and product performance across different countries and cities.

The goal of this project is to transform raw sales data into **clear and actionable business insights** that support **data-driven decision making**.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives
- Monitor overall **sales performance**
- Identify **top-performing products**
- Analyze **monthly sales trends**
- Compare **sales performance across countries**
- Provide **interactive filters for deeper exploration**

---

## 📊 Dashboard Metrics

The dashboard includes several **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**:

- **Total Sales:** 103K  
- **Total Orders:** 100  
- **Total Quantity Sold:** 146  
- **Number of Customers:** 12  
- **Number of Products:** 18  

These KPIs provide a quick summary of the overall business performance.

---

## 📈 Dashboard Analysis

### 1️⃣ Sales Trend by Month
This visualization shows how sales change across the months of the year.  
It helps identify:
- High-performing months
- Sales drops
- Potential seasonal trends.

### 2️⃣ Sales by Country
The dashboard compares revenue generated in different countries:

| Country | Sales |
|-------|------|
| United States | 66K |
| China | 31K |
| Germany | 3K |
| India | 3K |

The **United States** is the top-performing market.

### 3️⃣ Sales by Product
Product-level analysis highlights the best-selling products:

- Dell XPS 15 – 25K  
- ThinkPad X1 – 13K  
- Galaxy S24 – 11K  
- HP Spectre – 11K  
- iPad Air 6 – 10K  

This helps businesses identify products that generate the most revenue.

### 4️⃣ Quantity by Country
This chart shows the number of units sold in each country.  
The **United States leads in total quantity sold**, followed by China and other markets.
